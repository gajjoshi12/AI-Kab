How fast can we go live?
Timeline depends on the complexity of your stack, but most businesses go live within a few weeks. We start with a plug-in and audit phase, then train the AI on your brand, products, and team before launch.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is stored in encrypted, audited infrastructure with role-based access controls. Your data is never used to train public AI models. SOC 2 Type II attestation in progress, and we support HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA compliance requirements.
Will customers know they're talking to AI?
Kab identifies as your AI assistant by default (trust + compliance). Clients consistently prefer it — faster replies, zero hold times, and always-available support.
What if my team hates it?
They won't. AI Kab handles the repetitive work (data entry, follow-ups, scheduling, FAQ) so your team can do the high-value work they were hired for. We've yet to have a team ask for it to be turned off.
Do I need to change my existing tools?
No. We integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, Shopify, Stripe, Zendesk, Slack, and 100+ other platforms. If your tool has an API, we'll connect it — usually within hours.
What's the ROI guarantee?
If AI Kab doesn't recover its own monthly fee within the agreed timeline, we work for free until it does. We stand behind our product — ask us about the specifics on the strategy call.
